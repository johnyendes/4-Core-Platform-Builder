Bot Builder Pack v1

Purpose:
  Generate complete AI bot blueprints including:
  - Bot core definition
  - Intents list
  - Response mappings
  - Bot actions/integrations
  - Step-by-step conversation flow

Usage:
  1) generate_bot_core()
  2) generate_bot_intents()
  3) generate_bot_responses()
  4) generate_bot_actions()
  5) generate_bot_flow()
  6) Pass all outputs to bot_builder_gaps.connect()

The result is a complete bot architecture that can be converted into a chatbot, automation, workflow bot, or customer support agent.
